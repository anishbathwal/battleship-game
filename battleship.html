⚓ BATTLESHIP
Command your fleet. Destroy the enemy.

How to Play

  • Place your ships — Drag from the dock onto your board. Press R or click Rotate to rotate. Click a placed ship to pick it up.
  • Fire! — Click cells on Enemy Waters. Red = hit, white = miss, dark red = sunk.
  • Win — Sink all 5 enemy ships before the AI sinks yours.

Ships: The Devin (5), The Windsurf (4), The Cascade (3), The SWE-1.5 (3), The ACU (2)
